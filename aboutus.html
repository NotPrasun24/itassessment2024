The best in the Game: Hublot

Foundation and History:

Hublot was founded in 1980 by Italian watchmaker Carlo Crocco. The brand's name means "porthole" in French, which inspired the design of its first watch.

Design and Innovation:

Hublot is renowned for its "Art of Fusion" philosophy, blending traditional watchmaking techniques with modern materials. The brand was the first to combine natural rubber with precious metals in its watch designs.

Iconic Collections:

Big Bang: Launched in 2005, the Big Bang collection became an instant hit, featuring bold designs and innovative materials. Classic Fusion: This collection offers a more understated and elegant design while still incorporating Hublot's signature fusion of materials. Spirit of Big Bang: Introduced in 2014, this line features a tonneau (barrel) case shape.

Collaborations and Partnerships :

Hublot uses a variety of materials, including ceramic, titanium, gold, and sapphire. The brand has developed unique materials such as Magic Gold (scratch-resistant gold) and brightly colored ceramics.

Leadership:

Jean-Claude Biver played a significant role in Hublot's success when he joined the company as CEO in 2004. He is credited with revitalizing the brand and driving its growth.
